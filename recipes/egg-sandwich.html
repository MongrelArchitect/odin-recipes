Egg Sandwich

Description

Usually for breakfast but good any time of day, this simple sandwich is satisfying, tasty and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 slice cheese (cheddar preferably)
  • 2 pieces of bread (sourdough is best)
  • A bit of butter for cooking
  • Salt and pepper
  • A bit of mayonaise

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet to medium and melt a bit of butter inside
  2. Toast bread to desired level
  3. Spread mayo on toast
  4. Crack two eggs in the skillet and break the yolk
  5. Flip eggs over once white is cooked
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste
  7. Add slice of cheese to one egg until melty
  8. Remove eggs from skillet and place on toast
  9. ENJOY!
