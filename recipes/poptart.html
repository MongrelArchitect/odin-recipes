Pop-Tarts

Description

A classic processed-food breakfast! Rectangular-shaped toaster pastry with a sweet filling sealed inside, usually frosted. The best flavor is clearly s'mores - any others are obviously inferior.

Ingredients

  • 1 package Kellogg's brand Pop-Tarts (preferably s'mores flavor)

Steps

  1. Open outer cardboad box
  2. Remove individual Pop-Tarts package
  3. Open package and remove Pop-Tarts
  4. (Optional) Toast Pop-Tarts to desired level
  5. ENJOY!
